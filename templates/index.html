What is this?

AiTrader is a website that helps hobby traders idenitify stocks that will outperform the market after earnings announcements.

How does it work?

An algorithm based on machine learning identifies inefficiencies in the market and makes predictions about future movements based on passed observations.

Why does this work?

Earnigns announcements have a tendency to affect stock movements for several days or even months after announcement. This phenomenom is called Post Earnings Announcement Drift and is both statistically and econmically viable.

Who is this for?

These predictions are short term and are more suited for hobby traders than large institations, as they would like to hold investments for longer than a year.
